Business Intelligence 8

SUPPLY CHAIN MANAGEMENT OPTIMIZATION

The project, conducted as part of the Business Intelligence 8 contest, focused on sales and supply chain management (SCM) data reporting, understanding the company's SCM base composition, and providing insights and recommendations for optimization. It involved data wrangling, exploratory data analysis, and the use of a Logistic Regression model in Python to identify factors affecting late delivery. Advanced SQL queries and procedures were developed for data ingestion, and shipment routes were analyzed and visualized using Power BI to enhance delivery optimization.
